Our Story

A Legacy Built on
Trust & Results

"Education is the most powerful tool for social change."

Founded in 2003 at Civil Lines, Katni, Prerna Institute began with a singular vision — to make quality education accessible to every aspiring student in Central India. Under the leadership of Mr. Nandu Lal Vishwakarma, what started as a small classroom has grown into a powerhouse that has shaped thousands of careers.

In 2012, we expanded to our current location, bringing state-of-the-art facilities, expert faculty, and a system that combines conceptual clarity with competitive exam mastery.
